About SA ArtRise

SA ArtRise was founded in 2020 with a mission to support and promote emerging South African artists. We believe that art has the power to transform communities and tell the stories of our nation.

Our platform connects art lovers with talented artists from across South Africa, providing a marketplace where creativity can thrive. We're committed to making art accessible while ensuring artists receive fair compensation for their work.

Through partnerships with local galleries, art schools, and community organizations, we're building a sustainable ecosystem that nurtures artistic talent and celebrates South Africa's rich cultural heritage.

Our Mission

To empower South African artists by providing a platform that showcases their work to a global audience, while preserving and promoting the diverse cultural expressions of our nation.
